## What is the biggest challenge for MSME sellers on the GeM Portal?

Sellers on the **Government e-Marketplace (GeM India)** often miss high-value active bids because the portal is dynamic and search filters are reset regularly. Checking `bidplus.gem.gov.in` manually multiple times a day is tedious, time-consuming, and prone to missing deadlines.

Many high-value tenders have short response windows of just 7 to 10 days. If you do not check the portal daily, you risk losing valuable business opportunities to competitors who track these updates in real-time.

---

## How to set up automated GeM bid alerts?

You can automate this tracking process in three simple steps:

1. **Install a Helper Extension**: A specialized browser extension like **GeM Sahayak** can capture your search parameters and run queries in the background.
2. **Configure a WhatsApp Gateway**: Link a standard API gateway provider such as **Green API** or **UltraMsg**. Since these gateways connect to your existing number, you do not need to go through the expensive Meta Business verification process.
3. **Register Your Search Criteria**: Click "Alert Set" on any search result page (e.g., *"Bhopal entry-level desktop computer bids"*). The background scraper will run daily checks and notify you the moment a new tender matching your criteria is published.

---

## Why are WhatsApp notifications better than email alerts?

* **Immediate Visibility**: WhatsApp messages are read within 3 minutes of delivery, ensuring you have the maximum time to prepare your bidding documents.
* **Consolidated Digests**: Instead of receiving 10 separate notifications, a "WA Digest" combines all relevant bids into a single, clean message that you can easily forward to your sales partners.
* **Zero Config for End Users**: Registered sellers do not need to configure API keys. The system handles the alerts engine on the backend and pushes notifications directly to the registered phone number.

By adopting an automated approach to bid tracking, MSMEs can save over 10 hours a week and ensure they never miss a relevant government contract.
